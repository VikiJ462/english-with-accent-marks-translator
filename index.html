English Translator with Accent Marks

This tool adds accent marks to English text based on simple pronunciation rules:

  • BREVE (˘) - Silent letters
  • OVERDOT (˙) - Schwa
  • MACRON (¯) - Long vowel
  • MACRON BELOW (̱) - Emphasis (stress)
  • GRAVE (`) - Homographs (e.g., to distinguish meanings)
  • DIAERESIS (¨) - Vowel change (separate pronunciation)

Note: This is a basic implementation with pattern-based rules. It may not cover all cases accurately due to English's irregularities.
